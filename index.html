Short Bio
Guoqing Liu is a Senior Researcher at Microsoft Research AI for Science. His research interests lie in reinforcement learning (RL), intersection of foundation models and decision making/RL, and their applications in scientific discovery. He has extensive experience in the research and application of deep RL. Notably, in Project Suphx, he and his team developed Suphx, a world-class Mahjong AI that achieved 10 DAN status on the Tenhou platform in mid-2019. In Project Mariana, he led the development of Inspector, the first general pixel-based automated game testing agent, which was deployed by Xbox Studios Quality. Recently, his work has centered on exploring the applications of large language/foundation models and RL agents in drug discovery (e.g., PDVN, RetroChimera, NaturLM). Before joining Microsoft Research, he earned his Ph.D. degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2021 through a joint PhD program between USTC and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA), under the supervision of Dr. Tie-Yan Liu and Prof. Nenghai Yu.
