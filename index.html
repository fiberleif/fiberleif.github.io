Short Bio
Guoqing Liu is a Researcher of Microsoft Research AI4Science. Before joining MSR, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) under the joint Ph.D. program between MSRA and USTC in 2021, advised by Prof. Tie-Yan Liu and Prof. Nenghai Yu. His work focuses on artificially intelligent agents based on reinforcement learning (RL). In Project Suphx, his team built the world-best Mahjong AI, named Suphx, which achieved 10 DAN on the Tenhou platform in mid 2019. In Project Mariana, he built the first general pixel-based automated game testing agent/tool, named Inspector, with Xbox Studios Quality. Most recently, he focuses on RL for scientific discovery.
