Biography
Guoqing Liu is a Senior Researcher at Microsoft Research AI for Science. His research interests encompass reinforcement learning (RL), the integration of large language models (LLMs) with RL (e.g., reasoning and natural language agents), and applications in drug discovery. He gained extensive experience in deep RL research and its real-world applications (e.g., Project Suphx: Mahjong AI, Project Mariana: Game Testing) during his Ph.D. studies and his first year as a researcher at Microsoft Research Asia. More recently, his work has focused on LLM reasoning, RL agents, and their applications in drug discovery (e.g., PDVN, RetroChimera, NatureLM, and MuProtein). Prior to joining Microsoft Research, he earned his Ph.D. from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) through a joint program with Microsoft Research Asia (2016-2021), supervised by Tie-Yan Liu and Nenghai Yu.
