Biography
Guoqing Liu is a Senior Researcher at Microsoft Research AI for Science based in Cambridge, UK. His research interests include reinforcement learning (RL), large language models (LLMs), and AI for Scientific Discovery. He develops LLMs and RL agents to advance chemistry and drug discovery, with representative works including PDVN, RetroChimera, NatureLM, and MuProtein. Previously, he was a Researcher and Joint PhD student at Microsoft Research Asia, where he conducted extensive research on reinforcement learning and its applications to video games, with representative works including Project Suphx: The World's Best Mahjong AI and Project Mariana: Automated Game Testing with Xbox Studios. He completed his Ph.D. from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) through a joint program with Microsoft Research Asia (2016-2021), under the supervision of Tie-Yan Liu and Nenghai Yu.
